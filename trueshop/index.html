About Application
This is an application developed for helping the users to know about the pricing of various commodities in various markets across India. This application also gives details about items across different e-commerce sites also, So that users will get the items sold out in different e-commerce platforms under one roof and can check the prices and can buy the needed item. This application will help users to crosscheck pricing of items in various markets and to get items in less cost.
